Hello. My name is Chenise Summer Paquette. I am a left-handed female Canadian designer from Ottawa ON. I originally intended on being a professional photographer but through my studies I didn't feel creatively fulfilled. This realization lead me to exploring and adventuring into the deep realm of creative thinking and conceptual design. Educated at Algonquin College for Graphic Design I am experienced in illustration - interactive design - web design - photography - logo design.
