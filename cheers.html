Cheers! - The app that gets you on track!

This app makes your celebrations, weekends andight-outs inexpensive and convenient. With a touch of a button you can locate all the bars, pubs and clubs nearest to you. This app will walk you right to the menu, just say Cheers! This product will help users explore and discover specific alcoholic beverages and specials by searching and locating in the specified radius of their location. The app shows you what pub/bar/restaurants around your current location has specific beverages or good specials on. The user is able to use keep a log of their favourite, desired or suggested beverages or locations. This organized account will keep you constantly updated on the selection and weekly specials related to your account and your current location.
